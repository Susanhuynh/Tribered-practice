What we are working on

Moomin Move



Moomin Move is a location-based, augmented reality mobile game for iOS and Android. We’re soft launching Moomin Move through unique adventures, with the first one launched at London’s Kew Gardens’ Easter event, followed by Silja Line cruises in Finland in July. More adventures will be introduced in the coming months before launching the core game later.

What we stand for

GAMES EVERYWHERE



We believe tomorrow’s games will be everywhere. They’ll intertwine the digital world with reality, changing how we play and live in exciting ways. Games will draw content from the real world through locations, augmented reality, social media, and the internet of things. But gamification will also change how we act in the real world.


We are a startup in the heart of Helsinki, building a platform that allows for integrating real-world actions and achievements into games. We believe this can be an entirely new way of monetizing content.
